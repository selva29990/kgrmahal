About KGR MAHAL

Welcome to our beautiful KGR Mahal! Our venue is the perfect setting for your dream wedding. With breathtaking views, elegant decor, and top-notch service, we offer everything you need to make your special day unforgettable. Our venue can accommodate both small intimate weddings and large extravagant affairs. Our experienced event coordinators will work with you to ensure every detail is taken care of, from catering to decor and entertainment. We offer a variety of customizable wedding packages to fit your needs and budget. Our spacious wedding hall can accommodate up to 1000 guests and features a dance floor, stage, and state-of-the-art lighting and sound system as service. We also have a variety of outdoor spaces, including a picturesque garden and a grand patio, perfect for cocktail hour or outdoor ceremonies. We take pride in offering the highest level of service to our couples and their guests. Our team is dedicated to making your wedding day stress-free and enjoyable.
